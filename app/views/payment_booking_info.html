Payment and booking information

Vehicle test guidance

Refer to the information below once you have received email / postal confirmation that your vehicle test application has passed initial assessment.

To book, cancel or amend an appointment, contact the Booking Section on 0300 123 900 and select option one.

VAT information

Rate will change depending on the vehicle testing type:

  • statuary vehicle testing is zero rated, attracting 0% VAT;
  • voluntary vehicle testing is standard rated, attracting 20% VAT;
  • retained fees are standard rated, attracting 20% VAT.

Paying for your vehicle test(s)

To pay for your test with a credit / debit card:

  • contact the Booking Section on 0300 123 9000 and select option one;
  • when you are put through to a call operator, explain to them that you are paying for a vehicle test and quote your application reference number;
  • when asked, provide your Visa / Mastercard details including:
    • card number
    • CSV / CVV number
    • account holder / name on card;
  • the call operator will provide a cost breakdown of your entire order, confirming the fee required for your test(s).

You can also pay for your test with a pre-funded account (PFA) or cheque.

Booking your vehicle test(s)

  • You can book your test by contacting the Booking Section for vehicles that you are having tested at a DVSA Testing Facility (GVTS). The call operator will provide you with booking instructions over the phone.
  • You will need to book directly with the Private / Authorised Testing Facility (POTF / ATF) if you are having your vehicle(s) tested there.

Cancelling your vehicle test(s)

Should you wish to cancel your technical or specialist test application:

  • before it is released from processing to allow examination of the vehicle, the fee paid will be refunded;
  • after it has been processed and released to allow examination of the vehicle, a fee of £100 will be retained and the remaining amount refunded.

Should you wish to cancel your technical or specialist test application, or an allocated appointment time after an appointment has been given, then:

  • If you give more than three working days notice prior to the appointment date, then either:
    • you can request a later appointment (subject to a maximum of three appointments being allocated to any one vehicle application) or not;
    • a fee of £100 will be retained by DVSA and the remaining amount refunded to the payee.
  • If you give three working days or less notice prior to the appointment date then the whole fee will be forfeited.

Amending your vehicle test(s)

If you would like to keep an appointment time that has been allocated and change the vehicle nominated you can get a different vehicle tested at a previously appointed time (subject to a maximum of three appointments per application), provided:

  • both vehicles already have an appointment time allocated;
  • the appointment is booked using the same pre-funded account (PFA), or by the same applicant if paying by card;
  • the vehicle is the same test category/ type with the same test duration;
  • the change is made before midday on the working day before the appointment.

Changing your vehicle test location(s)

If you have made an appointment at a DVSA Testing Facility (GVTS) and then change it to a Private / Authorised Testing Facility (POTF / ATF), the appointment with the GVTS must be cancelled or you will be charged for failing to attend the appointment.

Additional information

  • If you do not present the nominated vehicle at the allocated time, the fee will be forfeit and you will need to submit a new application.
  • If a previously allocated appointment is not filled with another vehicle application, the above cancellation policy will apply.
  • Any test appointment changes DVSA has to make due to unforseen circumstances will not be counted as part of your test allocation.
