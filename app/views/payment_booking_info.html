Payment and booking information

To book, cancel or amend an appointment, contact the Booking Section on 0300 123 900 and select option one.

VAT information

Rate will change depending on the vehicle testing type:

  • statuary vehicle testing is zero rated, attracting 0% VAT
  • voluntary vehicle testing is standard rated, attracting 20% VAT
  • retained fees are standard rated, attracting 20% VAT

Paying for your vehicle test(s)

You can pay for your test with a credit/ debit card, pre-funded account (PFA) or cheque.

Credit/ debit card

To pay with a credit/ debit card:

  • contact the Booking Section on 0300 123 900 and select option one
  • provide the operator with your booking reference number

You need to provide the operator with your:

  • card number
  • security CSV / CVV number
  • account holder / name on card

Pre-funded account (PFA) or cheque

Refer to the instructions on your application form to pay with a PFA or cheque.

Booking your vehicle test(s)

Ensure you have paid for your test(s) before making a booking.

DVSA Goods Vehicle Testing Facility (GVTS)

To book your test at a GVTS:

  • contact the Booking Section on 0300 123 900 and select option one
  • provide the operator with your booking reference number

The operator will guide you through the booking process.

Privately Owned Testing Facility (POTF)

You must book direct with the POTF.

Cancelling your vehicle test(s)

You can make up to three appointment changes per application. DVSA will not refund the test fee paid if you cancel your test with three working days or less notice.

If you cancel your vehicle test application before DVSA release it from processing, then DVSA will:

  • refund the test fee paid
  • also keep a fee of £100 if you submitted an IVA application

If you cancel your vehicle test application within three working days before the test date, then DVSA will:

  • let you request a later appointment for your vehicle test
  • also keep a fee of £100 if you submitted an IVA application

Amending your vehicle test(s)

You can swap like-for-like vehicle test appointments, given:

  • both vehicles already have an appointment time allocated
  • you used the same method of payment to pay for both tests
  • the vehicle is the same test category and type with the same test duration
  • the change occurs before midday on the working day before the appointment
  • you can swap up to three appointments per application

Changing your vehicle test location(s)

DVSA Goods Vehicle Testing Facility (GVTS) to Privately Owned Testing Facility (POTF)

Ensure you have cancelled the GVTS booking before requesting to change to a POTF. This will make sure you are not charged for not attending the GVTS appointment.

DVSA Goods Vehicle Testing Facility (GVTS) to DVSA Goods Vehicle Testing Facility (GVTS)

You do not need to cancel the initial GVTS booking.

Privately Owned Testing Facility (POTF)

You must contact the POTF direct to change from a POTF to another test centre.

Further information

You need to be aware:

  • if you do not present the nominated vehicle at the allocated time, you must pay for and submit a new application
  • your three appointment swaps are not used if DVSA make changes to your appointment
